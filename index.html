Kinship does not end at species boundaries

Kinecology is a small LLC based in Michigan developing software that fosters eco-literacy and channels financial support toward conservation and restoration work.

Our Mission
01

Promote eco-literacy and a sense of kinship with the living world.

02

Promote social-ecological resilience.

03

Financially support organizations doing the work of (1) and (2) in their communities.

First Offering

NatureJournal.eco

Our first offering encourages time outdoors and off-screen while directly supporting conservation and restoration organizations: Nature journal software designed for paper journalers and slow, attentive time observing.
